About Us
Cheacin is a peer-to-peer mobile application. It will provide a platform for mountain bike rider rent or lend a bike to other users. The purpose of this application is creating a safety and reliable free market for mountain bike riders. The Cheacin project aims to provide a platform for users could access more bike rental resources with detailed information and good price.
